About this Sudoku

Sudoku is the world's most popular logic puzzle. Every board here is generated fresh and is guaranteed to have a single solution reachable by pure logic — no guessing. Pick your difficulty, or take the shared Daily Challenge and build a streak.

How to play

  1. Each row, each column and each 3×3 box must contain the digits 1–9, once each.
  2. Tap a cell, then tap a number — or use your keyboard.
  3. Duplicate numbers are highlighted so you can spot mistakes.
