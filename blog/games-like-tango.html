5. Trail

How the Tango rules work (quick refresher)

  • Every cell is one of two symbols.
  • No three identical symbols in a row, horizontally or vertically.
  • Each row and column has an equal count of both symbols.
  • = links two matching cells; ✕ links two that differ.
