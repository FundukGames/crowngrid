12 Best Games Like Wordle to Play Free in 2026

Updated June 2026

Wordle kicked off the daily-puzzle craze, but once you've solved today's word you're left wanting more. The good news: a whole genre of free daily puzzles has grown up around it. Here are twelve of the best games like Wordle — a mix of word, number and logic puzzles — and which ones you can play unlimited.

Word games like Wordle

  1. NYT Connections — sort sixteen words into four hidden groups. The most talked-about daily puzzle right now.
  2. Quordle — four Wordles at once, sharing your guesses across all boards.
  3. Contexto — guess the secret word; the game tells you how semantically "close" each guess is. Brutally addictive.
  4. Strands — a themed word-search twist from the NYT.

Number & logic games like Wordle

  1. Nerdle — Wordle with math: guess a hidden equation like 48-36=12.
  2. Sudoku — the timeless 1–9 logic puzzle. Our version is free, unlimited, and has easy-to-hard levels plus a daily challenge.
  3. CrownGrid — a Queens-style logic puzzle: one crown per region, row and column, none touching. Unlimited and no login.
  4. SunMoon — a Tango-style binary puzzle: balance ☀️ and 🌙 with no three in a row.
  5. Trail — draw one line through every cell, connecting the numbers in order.

Geography & trivia games like Wordle

  1. Globle — guess the mystery country; the globe warms up as you get closer.
  2. Worldle — identify a country from its silhouette.
  3. Heardle-style music games — name the song from a short intro clip.

The one thing Wordle won't give you

Almost every game on this list — Wordle included — caps you at one puzzle a day, and many now require an account.
