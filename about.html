About Funduk Games

Funduk Games is an independent studio making free, fast, no-login puzzle games you can play right in your browser. No downloads, no sign-up, no paywalls — just open a page and play.

Our games

  • CrownGrid — an unlimited crown-placement logic puzzle: place one crown in every region, row and column without any two touching. Includes a Daily Challenge with streaks. (Live now.)
  • SunMoon — a Tango-style binary logic puzzle: fill the grid with ☀️ and 🌙 under the no-three-in-a-row, balance and = / ✕ rules. (Live now.)
  • Sudoku — the classic 1–9 number puzzle, free and unlimited with easy/medium/hard levels and a daily challenge. (Live now.)
  • More daily puzzle games are in development and will appear here soon.

What we believe

Great puzzles should be free and instantly accessible. Our games are designed to be quick to pick up, satisfying to solve, and friendly on phones, tablets and desktops alike. We keep them free by showing ads — see our Privacy Policy for details.
