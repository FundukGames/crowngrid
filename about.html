About Funduk Games

Funduk Games is a tiny independent studio that makes the kind of games we reach for ourselves: quick, fair brain games you can open in a browser tab and finish on a coffee break. No downloads, no sign-up, no paywalls, and no "come back tomorrow" — just open a page and play.

The name comes from funduk, the hazelnut 🌰 — which is also why one of our games stars a squirrel hoarding nuts. We're small, we ship often, and we'd rather make ten polished little games than one bloated one.

Our games

  • CrownGrid — an unlimited crown-placement logic puzzle: place one crown in every region, row and column without any two touching. Includes a Daily Challenge with streaks. (Live now.)
  • SunMoon — a Tango-style binary logic puzzle: fill the grid with ☀️ and 🌙 under the no-three-in-a-row, balance and = / ✕ rules. (Live now.)
  • Sudoku — the classic 1–9 number puzzle, free and unlimited with easy/medium/hard levels and a daily challenge. (Live now.)
  • Trail — a one-line path puzzle: draw a single continuous line through every cell, connecting the numbers in order. (Live now.)
  • Klondike, Spider & FreeCell Solitaire — the three classic card games, with drag-and-drop, unlimited deals, undo and a daily challenge. (Live now.)
  • Nutfall — a 3D Plinko idle game: nuts rain down a tree trunk, bounce off the +/× modifiers and tornadoes you place, and pile up your harvest as you merge, upgrade and climb the levels. (Live now.)
  • More games are in development and will appear here soon.

What we believe

A great puzzle shouldn't put up a toll booth before the fun. Ours are built to be picked up in seconds, satisfying to crack, and comfortable on a phone, tablet or laptop alike — and every logic board is generated to have a single solution you can reach by reasoning, never by guessing. We keep the games free by showing ads; see our Privacy Policy for how that works.
