Contact us

Funduk Games is a small independent studio, and we genuinely read every message. Whether you've found a bug, have an idea for a new puzzle, or want to talk business, we'd love to hear from you.

Email

The fastest way to reach us is by email:

contact@fundukgames.com

We aim to reply to genuine messages within a few business days. To help us help you faster, please include as much detail as you can.

What to write about

  • Bug reports. Tell us which game (CrownGrid, Sudoku, SunMoon, Trail, Klondike, Spider, FreeCell or Nutfall), what device and browser you used, and what happened versus what you expected. A screenshot is hugely helpful.
  • Feedback & ideas. Too easy, too hard, a control that feels off, or a game you'd love us to build next — it all shapes what we make.
  • Daily Challenge issues. If a daily board won't load or your streak looks wrong, let us know the date and game.
  • Business & advertising. For partnership, advertising or press enquiries, email us with "Business" in the subject line.
  • Privacy requests. Questions about data and cookies are covered in our Privacy Policy; email us if anything is unclear.

Follow the journey

We keep an honest indie-dev blog about building Funduk Games — what works, what flops, and the business side of going solo — on Patreon. You can also read strategy guides and round-ups on our blog.

A quick note on support

All of our games run entirely in your browser with no account and no login, so there's no password to reset and nothing to recover server-side. Your progress, streaks and best times are saved locally in your browser. If you clear your browser data or switch devices, that local progress won't carry over — this is by design and keeps the games private and instant.
