What is SunMoon?

SunMoon is a browser-based binary logic puzzle in the same family as Tango. Each cell holds one of two symbols — a ☀️ sun or a 🌙 moon. Solve it with pure deduction: no guessing required, and every board has a single logical solution.

Unlike puzzles locked to one per day, SunMoon is unlimited — tap "New puzzle" for a fresh board any time, or take the shared Daily Challenge and build a streak.

The rules

  1. Each cell is a ☀️ or a 🌙. Tap to cycle ☀️ → 🌙 → empty.
  2. No three in a row — horizontally or vertically.
  3. Each row and column has an equal number of ☀️ and 🌙.
  4. A = between two cells means they match; an ✕ means they differ.

Quick solving tips

SunMoon is cracked by two simple ideas working together:

  • No triples. Two identical symbols side by side force the opposite symbol on both ends; a lone gap between two identicals must be the opposite too.
  • Balance. Each row and column holds equal suns and moons — once half are placed, the rest of that line is forced.
  • Clues chain. An = locks a matching pair, an ✕ locks opposites; combine them with the no-triple rule for fast cascades.
